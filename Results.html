PM2.5 and [Second Map/Result] Annual Averages (2013-2022)

Yearly aggregated PM2.5 and [Second Map/Result] concentration maps for Bosnia and Herzegovina, derived from CAMS NetCDF data and processed as annual means. Each map shows the spatial distribution for a given year, reprojected and clipped to the study area.

  • Data source: CAMS European Air Quality Reanalysis Dataset (2013–2022) and [Second Source]
  • Processing: Monthly to annual aggregation, reprojection, and clipping
  • Visualization: PNG maps, 10 years, slider navigation
